Build your message

What to do next

  1. Find your House district if you do not know it (find your district).
  2. Search for your 2026 candidates.

    Try "2026 House candidates [your district number and state]" or "2026 Senate candidates [your state]" in a search engine.

    Then search "[candidate name] campaign 2026".

  3. Open each candidate's official campaign website.
  4. Find the contact page.

    Look for "Contact," "Email," "Get in touch," or "Contact the campaign."

  5. Paste your message and send.

    Add one sentence if you can. Send it to one candidate or all of them.

Use campaign contact pages, not official government office pages, when asking campaign questions.

After you send it

Share this page where people are already talking about Ukraine. This includes YouTube comments, Reddit, Facebook, group chats, or others who care about Ukraine.

No signup. No tracking. No database. No candidate list. No endorsements. This page does not send anything for you. You copy, edit, and send the message yourself.
