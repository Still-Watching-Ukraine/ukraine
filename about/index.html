About this site

Why we built this

We built this tool because we care deeply about Ukraine and wanted a simple way to help more people speak up.

Candidates are asking for votes. Voters can ask them where they stand.

This tool is meant to make that first step easier: choose a message, type one sentence in your own words, copy it, and send it through a campaign contact page.

The point is not to endorse anyone or rate anyone. The point is to make more candidates hear that Ukraine matters to voters.

Slava Ukraini.

Your privacy

Messages are assembled entirely in your browser. Nothing you write leaves your device. The site does not send messages for you. You copy, personalize, and send the final message yourself.

This is a static page. There is no database, no account system, and no server-side message storage. Check the page source if you want to see how it works.

Not affiliated with anyone

This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any YouTube channel, organization, PAC, campaign, or government office. No message generated here speaks for anyone but you. That's the point.
